AI Philosophy & Research Journey

An enthusiastic researcher in the field of Artificial Intelligence, with a focus on deep learning and computer vision. My fascination lies in exploring how neural networks, even at the level of small artificial neurons, can solve complex problems like image recognition and natural language processing. The potential of these technologies truly amazes me!

🚀 My research goals include:

  • Identify how machines actually learn and address the black-box nature of deep learning.
  • Building models that require less data and less computational energy (Sustainable AI models).
  • Developing models that achieve optimal results without specialized hardware.
